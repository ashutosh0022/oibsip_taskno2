Tribute page to Cristiano Ronaldo

- A genius in Football -

  • Winning his first Ballon d'Or in 2008, marking the start of his domination of the award with five more wins to follow.
  • Scoring his first hat-trick for Manchester United against Newcastle United in 2008, cementing his status as one of the world's best players.
  • Scoring a stunning goal against Porto in the UEFA Champions League quarterfinals in 2009, which is widely regarded as one of his greatest ever goals.
  • Leading Real Madrid to four UEFA Champions League titles in five years between 2014 and 2018, including scoring two goals in the 2017 final against Juventus.
  • Winning the European Championship with Portugal in 2016, his country's first major international trophy.
  • Scoring his 500th career goal in 2015, becoming the fastest player in history to reach that milestone.
  • Scoring his 700th career goal in 2019, becoming the first player in history to reach that milestone.
  • Breaking the record for most goals scored in a single UEFA Champions League season with 17 goals in 2014.
  • Scoring his 100th goal for Portugal in 2018, becoming the first player in history to reach that milestone.

"I'm not a perfectionist, but I like to feel that things are done well. More important than that, I feel an endless need to learn, to improve, to evolve, not only to please the coach and the fans but also to feel satisfied with myself." -Cristiano Ronaldo
